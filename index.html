About The Project

The Drowsiness Detection System is an innovative solution designed to enhance road safety by preventing accidents caused by driver fatigue. Our system uses cutting-edge computer vision and machine learning techniques to monitor drivers in real-time.

According to the National Highway Traffic Safety Administration, drowsy driving causes more than 100,000 crashes annually in the U.S. alone. Our solution aims to significantly reduce these numbers by providing timely alerts to drivers showing signs of fatigue.

The system analyzes multiple factors including eye closure duration, blink frequency, head position, and facial expressions to accurately detect drowsiness. When potential fatigue is detected, the system triggers a series of escalating alerts to ensure the driver's attention.
